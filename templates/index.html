Welcome to Leonardo Guzman's game review website!

The website is about writting reviews for videogames. You need to add yourself as a player and also add the game you want to review. To achieve this here are the steps:

Step 1: Add a player by clicking in the "Add Player" button in the menu, enter all the fields and hit "Submit". Now go back to the main menu by hitting "Menu".

Step 2: Add a game by clicking in the "Add Game" button and follow the same steps as instructed in step 1.

Step 3: Add your review by clicking in the "Add Review" button and follow the same steps as instructed in step 1 and step 2.

And you are done! Congratulation you added the review of your game. You can check your player info, game, and review by hitting: "View Player", "View Game" and "View Review" in the Menu.

You can also search a player or game by genre using the "Search Player By Genre" and "Search Game By Genre" buttons.
